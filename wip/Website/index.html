TTWS

The Things We Say is a online multiplayer game based on the game Words That Follow

I'm a big fan of the game and wanted to try and re-create it

Designed With

  • Javascript
  • PHP
  • MySQL
  • AJAX
Game Play

Players are presented with a random image which they must write a caption for

Next players vote on the best caption. They can't vote for their own and must vote to earn points

The person with the most points at the end of 10 rounds wins
